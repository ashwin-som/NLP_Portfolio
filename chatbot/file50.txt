Star Wars: Young Jedi Adventures is an animated Star Wars series focused for preschoolers, grade schoolers, and their families. It is set during the High Republic Era.[1] It will release on Disney+ with shorts on April 26, 2023[3] and on Disney Junior and Disney+ on May 4, 2023.[2]

Set during The High Republic era, the animated series follows Jedi younglings as they study the ways of the Force, explore the galaxy, help citizens and creatures in need, and learn valuable skills needed to become Jedi along the way.[3]

On May 27, 2022, during Celebration Anaheim, the series was announced.[1] On February 9, 2023, the cast was revealed, and it was announced that the series will premiere on May 4, 2023.[2]
