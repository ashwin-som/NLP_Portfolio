The Acolyte is a Star Wars television series currently being developed by Leslye Headland for Disney+. Headland will write, executive produce, and serve as the series' showrunner. The show will focus on the emergence of dark side powers in the final days of the High Republic Era.

The Acolyte is a mystery-thriller that will take viewers into a galaxy of shadowy secrets and emerging dark-side powers in the final days of the High Republic era. A former Padawan reunites with her Jedi Master to investigate a series of crimes, but the forces they confront are more sinister than they ever anticipated.[2]

The series logo from the 2020 Disney Investor Day presentation
On April 22, 2020, Variety reported that a Star Wars series was in development for Disney+, with Russian Doll co-creator Leslye Headland writing and serving as showrunner. The report noted that the series will be female-centric, and that it will take place in a new part of the Star Wars timeline.[6] The series was officially announced on May 4, 2020 on StarWars.com. The announcement also revealed that Headland will be an executive producer on the project.[1] The series' title, logo, and general premise were revealed at a Disney investors meeting on December 10, 2020.[7] Rayne Roberts will co-develop the series with Headland.[8] On July 22, 2022, Amandla Stenberg and Star Wars jointly announced on Instagram the former's casting in the series.[3] On November 7, 2022, it was announced that the series started production in the United Kingdom, and several cast members were revealed.[2]
